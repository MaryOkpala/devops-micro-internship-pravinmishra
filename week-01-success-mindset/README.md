# Reflection – Week 1

### Biggest insight I got about myself this week

I realized that I learn best when I understand the "why" behind a concept instead of just memorizing definitions. Breaking complex topics into simple, real-life examples helped me build confidence. I also realized that I enjoy documenting my learning journey and sharing what I learn with others.

### My biggest weakness/loop I noticed

I sometimes overthink concepts because I want to understand everything perfectly before moving on. This can slow my progress. I've realized that it's okay to keep learning gradually and revisit difficult topics as I gain more experience.

### One system I will implement from this week (exact habit + time)

Focused DevOps learning with my phone on Do Not Disturb. At the end of each study session, I will spend 10 minutes reviewing what I learned and writing down one key takeaway.
